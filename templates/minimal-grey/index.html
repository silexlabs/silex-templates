About this template

This is a free template for Silex, live web creation, the free website builder for you, designers and web designers. It looks slick and professional, this is great for business portfolio, to engage new customers.

Customize it for your website

Download the zip file, and unzip it to a folder in your dropbox. 

Then open Silex in a browser and then open the template file : "editable.html".

Then insert your content, drag, drop, modify the design and publish it online. There it is your website!

How to contribute

These pages are made without programming, just drag and drop, with Silex, live web creation. So feel free to make it better and send us back the template.
